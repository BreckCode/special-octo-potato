AI-Powered Nudging Engine
Meeting Notes — Strategy Discussion
Context & Problem Statement
CodeYogi ek non-profit foundation hai jo bacchon ko online padhata hai (WhatsApp bot + chat app ke zariye).

Mentor-student ratio bahut challenging hai:
Ek mentor ke paas ~500 students hote hain (5 cohorts x 100 bacche)
Mentor sirf mahine mein ek baar call karta hai har cohort se — yaani 5 ghante/mahina
Problem: Ek mentor itne bacchon ko personally nudge nahi kar sakta manually
Insight: Bacche tab tak mehnat nahi karte jab tak koi adult unpar believe na kare aur push na kare
Self-discipline sabme nahi hoti — external motivation zaruri hai

Goal: AI Nudging Engine
Mentor ke behalf par AI automatic messages bheje jo personal lage — jaise mentor khud likh raha ho.

Messages feel hone chahiye ki 'Ankit sir mujh par personally dhyan de rahe hain'
Scope: Pehle app focus (WhatsApp baad mein)
Human-in-the-loop bhi possible ho zaroort ke hisaab se

Key Features Discussed
Personalization Layers
Student ki activity dekh ke reaction dena (e.g., thumbs up for good work)
Agar koi 3 din se kuch nahi kar raha — reason poochhna, follow-up karna
Personal details yaad rakhna:
Papa ki tabiyat kharab thi — uske baare mein poochhna
Ghar ke events, personal struggles track karna
Location-based events use karna:
Example: Dehradun mein baadh aayi — wahan ke students ko context-aware message bhejna
World/local events ko messages se connect karna

Message Types
Encouragement messages (good work par positive reinforcement)
Re-engagement messages (inactive students ke liye)
Follow-up messages (student ke reply ke basis par)
Check-in messages (personal events ke baad)
Event-triggered messages (local news, world events se inspired)

Advanced Personalization (Future)
Voice cloning — mentor ki awaaz mein audio message bhejna
Har student ke liye unique message style (mentor ka tone maintain ho)

Immediate Next Steps (Action Items)

Message delivery mechanism

What the Strategy Doc Should Cover
High-level workflow (not technical architecture)
Types of messages aur unke triggers
Data requirements (assume data available hai)
Personalization strategies
Pitfalls & risks:
AI galat ya inappropriate message bhej sakta hai
Over-messaging se students irritate ho sakte hain
Fake personal touch feel ho sakta hai agar zyada generic ho
Quality assurance mechanisms
Phased rollout:
Phase 1: Easy wins (basic nudges, activity-based triggers)
Phase 2: Personalization layer (memory, follow-ups)
Phase 3: Advanced (voice clone, local events, world news integration)

Important Constraints
Abhi ek bhi line code nahi likhna
Database, APIs, service integration — in details mein nahi jaana abhi
Focus: Strategy, workflow, message types, personalization ideas
Approach: Maan lo data available hai — sirf use case design karo
