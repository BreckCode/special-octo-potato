**Platform:**  
CodeYogi ka platform ek mobile app hai, jisme courses ki chat, channels, aur group features hain. WhatsApp/Telegram integrations bhi possible ho sakte hain, lekin core engagement apne app par hoti hai.

**Students & Groups:**  
App me millions of students ho sakte hain, lekin har group me level-wise segmentation hoti hai. Har group me max 100 students hote hain, jo similar level pe hain. Jaise hi kisi group me 100 students ho jate hain, naya group ban jata hai usi level ke liye. Har student apne level ke relevant group me hi hota hai.

**Courses:**  
Sirf coding nahi, multiple types ke courses ho sakte hain – jaise AI, Computer basics, ya kuch aur. Levels ka matlab: courses ke modules, chapters, video lectures, assignments, ya quizzes – course design pe depend karta hai.

**Data & Tracking:**  
Students ki progress, activity, participation, sab ka sufficient data available hai (assume karo LMS ya backend system integrated hai). App me har cheez track hoti hai, isliye AI nudging engine ko jo bhi student engagement signals chahiye honge, wo available hain.

**AI Mentor ka Concept:**  
AI Mentor ka matlab hai – har student ko ek human mentor assigned hai, par real human sirf kabhi-kabhi message karega (monthly calls ya jab zarurat ho). Roz marra ki nudges, push messages, appreciation – yeh sab AI karega, lekin aise, jaise wohi unka asli mentor ho. AI messages bhi mentor ke account se aur uski hi chat style me jayenge – taki student ko hamesha lage real mentor hi de raha hai time aur attention.

**Budget/Resources:**  
Budget ka constraint nahi hai – AI ko full tarike se use kar sakte hain. Objective ye hai ki bachche ko kabhi bhi doubt na ho ki unke saath machine interact kar rahi hai, unko pure human touch lage.

**Current System:**  
Abhi app me mentors ka concept simple hai – mainly chat, channels, groups. Aur koi advanced nudging system ya AI-based personalization nahi hai. Ye poori strategy scratch se sochni hai – implementation zero se start hogi.

**Priority:**  
Sabse pehle focus yeh hai:

1. Real mentor ki chat style ko AI me replicate karna
2. Automated, personalized nudges – student ko lagna chahiye ki real mentor soch samajh ke likh raha hai
3. Group & personal messages dono me natural human-like experience
4. Human mentor ke liye handoff system (AI + Human coordination)  
   Ye 3–4 cheezein phase 1 priority hain.

**Direct Messaging Constraint:**  
 Student directly mentor ko personal message nahi kar sakta hai. Jab tak mentor apne end par particular student ke liye chat window khud se open nahi karta, student apne aap initiate nahi kar sakta. Iska matlab: one-way initiation rights sirf mentor ke paas hain. Student group chats, broadcasts, ya scheduled check-ins ke through mentor ki baat sun sakta hai, lekin personal window tabhi active hoti hai jab mentor khud open kare.
