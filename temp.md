# AI Nudging Engine - Message Categories & Examples

---

## 1. Group Me Kya Bhejenge

### Regular/Scheduled Messages

- **Weekly/Daily Top Students:** Har week/day top performers ki list + sabse jyada progress karne wale students
- **Group Milestones:** "Itne students ne itni levels complete kar di he, next group me aa gaye he"
- **Welcome New Members:** Din me ek bar batch message — "Welcome our new friend [Name]"
- **Daily/Weekly Challenge:** Course se related predefined challenges group me daale
- **Quiz Challenge:** "Ek quiz ho jaye, dekhte he kisne kitna sikha" — limited time quiz, baad me results share kare
- **Reward Announce:** Kisi student ko reward mila to group me post kare

### Appreciation Messages

- **Help Appreciation:** Kisi ne group me kisi ki help ki to appreciate kare — "@Modi ne @Rahul ka doubt solve kiya, mentor material he ye to"
- **Solution React:** Jo student solution deta he uske message par react kare (thumbs up etc.)
- **Report Appreciation:** Student ne report ki aur verify ho gayi to group me mention kare (personal bhi ho sakta he)

### Engagement Messages

- **Mentor as Friend:** Group me ek dost ki tarah engage kare, hamesha course ki baat na ho
- **Student Showcase:** Student ne kuchh hatke kiya to usko showcase karne ka mauka de group me

## 2. Personaly Kya Bhejenge

### Onboarding & Setup

- **Welcome Message:** Student enroll kare tab — "Me tumhara mentor hun, is course me tumhe guide karunga"
- **Personal Questions:** "Konsi class me ho? Kya kar rahe ho?" — real mentor jaisa feel aaye
- **Course Context:** "X% students ye course Y time me complete karte he" — realistic expectations set kare
- **Late Night Active:** Koi late night padh raha he ya active he to appreciate kare — "Bhai raat ke 1 baj rahe he aur ye abhi bhi padh raha he"

### Progress-Based Messages

- **Progress Praise:** 3+ levels ek din me — "Bhai aaj 4 levels kar diye, kal se bhi zyada"
- **Progress Drop:** Aaj 2 levels, kal 4 thi — "Bhai aaj 2 hi levels ki he, kal 4 ki thi..."
- **Peer Comparison:** "Mene dekha tumhari progress kafi achchhi he, abhi tum 80% students se aage ho"
- **Milestone Celebration:** Level 10, 20, 30 etc par — "50 levels done! 80% log yaha tak nahi pahunchte"
- **Streak Tracking:** Duolingo jaisa — "Mene dekha tumne 12 din ki streak kar di he course karne ki"
- **Weekly Summary:** Personal weekly summary — "Tumne is hafte 10 levels ki, itna time diya, keep going"

### Learning & Content

- **Level-Based Tips:** Student ki current level ke hisab se tips de
- **Stuck Point Help:** Known difficult assignments par proactive help bheje
- **Related Content:** Course se related YouTube videos, docs, new releases bheje
- **Course-Related Questions:** "5 levels ho gayi, kya lagta he 5 aur kitne dino me kar loge?"
- **Personal Quiz:** "10 levels par aa gaye, kuchh test ho jaye? Ruko form ready karke bhejta hun"

### Personal Touch Messages

- **Birthday Wish:** Student ko birthday par wish kare
- **Mentor's Own Story:** "Mene bhi ek time par ye course kiya tha, mere sath bhi ye problem aayi thi, normal he"
- **Life Gyan:** Kabhi kabhi course se hat ke baat — "Agar subah course karna he aur Instagram open kar lete ho to homescreen me CodeYogi app rakh do"
- **Personal Check-in:** "Tumhare papa ki tabiyat kaisi he ab?"
- **Busy Excuse (Human Touch):** "Kal me thoda busy tha, nahi dekh paya tumhara message"

### Engagement Messages

- **Night Owl Appreciation:** "Bhai raat ke 1 baj rahe he aur tu abhi bhi padh raha he, dedication he"
- **Report Appreciation (Personal):** "Mene dekha tumne report ki, kafi achchha laga, tum CodeYogi ko improve karne me help kar rahe ho"
- **Showcase Prompt:** Student ne kuchh unique kiya to encourage kare share karne ke liye
- **Real Mentor Class Reminder:** "Mentor ki class kab he" wala reminder

### Timing & Delivery

- Student jab sabse jyada active hota he tabhi message bheje
- Mentor ke natural hours me hi bheje (mentor 11 PM ke baad message nahi karta to AI bhi nahi karega)
- 2-5 minute random delay rakhe
- Typing indicator 3-5 sec dikhaye
- Ek din me MAX 2 personal messages

## 3. Personal Reply Aane Par Kya Reply Denge (Student Ko Lage Sir Time De Rahe He)

### Reply Strategy — Student ko feel ho ki mentor personally time de raha he

**Core Principle:** Turant reply mat do. Thoda wait karo (1-5 min), typing indicator dikhao, phir reply do — jaise real mentor soch ke likh raha he.

### Simple Replies (ok, thanks, yes)

- Short aur warm reply do — "Haan bilkul, keep going"
- Ya ek reaction de do (thumbs up, fire emoji)

### Student Ne New Information Di (Dadaji bimar he, exams he)

- **Empathy dikhao + follow-up question puchho:**
  - Student: "Mere dadaji bimar he"
  - Mentor: "Arey kab se he? Kya hua unko?" ya "Oh... take care bhai, family pehle aati he"
  - Baad me follow-up: "Dadaji ki tabiyat kaisi he ab?"
- **Ye student ko lagega ki mentor ne yaad rakha he**

### Student Ne Promise Kiya (Kal se start karunga)

- Turant: "Achchha kal se pakka? Kitne baje se karoge start?" (time commit karvao)
- Next day: "Aaj ka din he bhai, tune khud bola tha kal"
- React bhi karo message par (thumbs up)

### Student Ne Reason Diya (Exam he, bimar hun)

- "Achchha exam kab tak he? Khatam hone de phir full power se shuru"
- Follow-up after exam date: "Exam khatam ho gayi kya?"

### Conversation Handling Rules

- **3+ back-and-forth ke baad:** Graceful exit — "Bhai mera ek aur group session he abhi, baad me baat karte he"
- **Emotional/Personal issue:** Real mentor ko forward karo — AI handle na kare
- **"Are you a bot?":** Turant real mentor ko flag karo
- **Long gap after student reply:** "Sorry bhai, abhi dekha tumhara message" (human jaisa lagega)

### Human-Like Reply Patterns

- Split messages me reply do:
  ```
  "Achchha..."
  "exam he tumhari"
  "kab tak he?"
  ```
- Kabhi kabhi context bhulne jaisa karo: "Haan tumne bataya tha... exam thi na?"
- Casual openers use karo: "Arey haan", "Achchha suno", "Btw"
- Kabhi thoda late reply do (5-15 min) — lagega mentor busy tha

## 4. Examples — Follow-up Chains (Puchha 3 Din Se Kuchh Nahi Kiya, Uske Baad Kya)

### Example 1: Student 3 Din Se Inactive

```
Day 3 (Drop-off Alert):
  Mentor: "Bhai kya hua? 3 din se dikhte nahi ho... sab theek he?"

Student replies: "Exam he sir"
  Mentor: "Achchha exam kab tak he?"

Student: "15 March tak"
  Mentor: "Achchha tension mat lo, 15 ke baad full power se shuru karte he"

16 March (Follow-up):
  Mentor: "Exam khatam ho gayi? Ab wapas start kare?"

Student: "Haan sir"
  Mentor: "Chalo phir aaj 2-3 levels se start karte he, dhire dhire speed aayegi"
```

### Example 2: Student Bola "Kal Se Start Karunga"

```
Student: "Sir kal se start karta hun"
  Mentor: "Pakka? Kitne baje se karoge?" + thumbs up react

Student: "Shaam ko 5 baje"
  Mentor: "Done, me check karunga"

Next day 6 PM (agar kuchh nahi kiya):
  Mentor: "Bhai 5 baje bola tha... kya hua?"

Agar kar liya:
  Mentor: "Mene dekha aaj start kar diya! Aise hi continue karo"
```

### Example 3: Student Bimar He

```
Student: "Sir bimar hun"
  Mentor: "Arey kya hua? Kab se he?"

Student: "Bukhar he 2 din se"
  Mentor: "Arey take care bhai. Pehle theek ho jao, course kahin nahi ja raha"

4-5 din baad:
  Mentor: "Kaisa he ab? Theek ho gaye?"

Student: "Haan sir"
  Mentor: "Chalo achchha hua. Jab man kare tab wapas start karo, no pressure"
```

### Example 4: Student Kuchh Nahi Bola (No Reply)

```
Day 3:
  Mentor: "Bhai kya hua? 3 din se kuchh nahi kiya..."

No reply for 2 days:
  Mentor: "Sab theek he na? Bas ek message kar de bhai"

Still no reply (flag to real mentor):
  Real mentor call kare ya voice note bheje
```

### Example 5: Student Progress Achchhi He

```
Mentor: "Bhai mene dekha aaj 5 levels kar di, kal se bhi zyada"
Student: "Haan sir aaj man lag gaya"
Mentor: "Aise hi karte raho. Tumhari district me top 5 me ho tum abhi"
Student: "Sachchi sir?"
Mentor: "Haan bhai, data bol raha he. Keep pushing"
```

### Example 6: Group Me Help Ki — Personal + Group Dono

```
Group me:
  Mentor: "@Rahul ne @Modi ka doubt solve kar diya, ye to mentor material he"
  + Rahul ke solution par react (thumbs up)

Personal me Rahul ko:
  Mentor: "Bhai mene dekha group me Modi ki help ki, bahut achchha laga. Aise hi karte raho"
```

## 5. Anti-Detection & Realism Rules

### Message Delivery Rules

- Kabhi bhi 2 students ko same minute me message mat bhejo
- Weekend par frequency kam karo
- Festivals/holidays par break lo (Diwali par mentor message nahi karega)
- Har student ko alag message aaye (copy-paste template nahi)
- AI messages real mentor ke messages ke beech me seamless fit ho

### Human Imperfections (Controlled)

- Kabhi kabhi typo daalo — "tumnhe" instead of "tumhe" (rare)
- Split messages bhejo — ek lambe ki jagah 2-3 chhote
- Kabhi late reply do — turant reply mat do har baar
- Context bhulne jaisa karo — "haan tumne bataya tha... exam thi na?"
- Casual openers — "arey haan", "suno ek baat", "btw"
- Reactions — thumbs up, fire emoji student ke messages par
- Rare: "Rahul... sorry Modi, ye tumhare liye tha" (galti se naam)
- Occasional voice notes — pre-recorded mentor voice milestones par

### Mentor Persona

- Real mentor ke 10-15 messages se persona build karo
- Tone (casual/formal), language (Hindi/Hinglish/English), emoji use, message length
- Common phrases capture karo ("dekh bhai", "no worries", "awesome!")
- Active hours match karo — mentor ki natural timing follow karo
